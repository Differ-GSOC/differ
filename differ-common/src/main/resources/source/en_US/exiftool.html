ExifTool is a platform-independent Perl library plus a command-line application for reading, writing and editing meta information in a wide variety of files.

ExifTool supports many different metadata formats including

  • EXIF
  • GPS
  • IPTC
  • XMP
  • JFIF
  • GeoTIFF
  • ICC Profile
  • Photoshop IRB
  • FlashPix
  • AFCP
  • ID3
as well as the maker notes of many digital cameras by Canon, Casio, FLIR, FujiFilm, GE, HP, JVC/Victor, Kodak, Leaf, Minolta/Konica-Minolta, Nikon, Nintendo, Olympus/Epson, Panasonic/Leica, Pentax/Asahi, Phase One, Reconyx, Ricoh, Samsung, Sanyo, Sigma/Foveon and Sony.
